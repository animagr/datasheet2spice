# Code of Conduct

`datasheet2spice` is an engineering project, but it should still be a humane
place to collaborate.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Critique models, assumptions, data, and code, not people.
- State uncertainty clearly when discussing extraction accuracy or simulator
  behavior.
- Help newcomers find a small next step.
- Respect datasheet, vendor model, and lab-data licensing boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing confidential datasheets, private lab data, or restricted vendor
  models without permission.
- Presenting generated starter models as safety-qualified or vendor-qualified
  models.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers ask to refocus.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block participants who
make collaboration unsafe or legally risky. If you need to report a problem,
open a private security advisory when sensitive material is involved, or contact
the maintainer through GitHub.
